Memberships & Working Groups

TERRITORIALITY & Cartographic Knowledge

Erfurt University, Germany

Member of the working group "Territoriality and Cartographic Knowledge" at Erfurt University, exploring the intersection of mapping practices, indigenous knowledge systems, and territorial transformations. Contributing to interdisciplinary research on how cartographic representations shape understandings of space and place in African and Asian contexts.

Ethio Think Tank Group

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Active member of the Ethio Think Tank Group, a collective of researchers and practitioners focused on Ethiopian development challenges. Contributing expertise in cultural heritage preservation, indigenous knowledge systems, and community-based development approaches. Participating in policy dialogues and providing evidence-based recommendations for sustainable development in Ethiopia.

Ethiopian Heritage Trust

National Organization

Dedicated member of the Ethiopian Heritage Trust, working to preserve and promote Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage. Currently managing the British Council's Cultural Protection Fund project for Dessie Museum Renovation. Collaborating with local communities to safeguard historical sites, traditions, and intangible cultural heritage through documentation, capacity building, and community engagement initiatives.

Community Engagement

Cultural Heritage Preservation

Leading community-based initiatives to document, protect, and promote cultural heritage sites and traditions. Working directly with Dawuro communities to safeguard the Great Dawuro Walls (Kati Halala Keela) and traditional music instruments like the Dinka. Developed training programs to raise community awareness and participation in heritage preservation, resulting in increased local ownership and sustainable conservation practices.
