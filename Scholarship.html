Research Focus & Interests

Cultural Studies

Ethnographic research, indigenous knowledge systems, and cultural heritage preservation with a focus on Ethiopian communities.

Development Policy

Analysis of development frameworks and their impact on local communities, inclusion policies, and marginalization issues.

GIS Mapping

Applying GIS technology to anthropological research, cultural heritage mapping, and spatial analysis of social phenomena.

Heritage Tourism Policy

Developing sustainable tourism policies that preserve cultural heritage while benefiting local communities.

Inclusion & Marginalization

Research on inclusive policies, social equity, and addressing marginalization in development contexts.

Impact Assessment

Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) for development projects, ensuring sustainable outcomes.
